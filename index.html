Hi.

We're a dedicated group of people working for the conservation of Australian water.
We believe that Australians have the right to water during a drought, so we've made a plan to conserve and hold water safely.
We've placed water collectors all throughout the edge of Australia.
That water then gets taken to a secure place inland and stored until the next drought, which we will then distribute to people in need.
People in need can sign up at www.signup-awco.glitch.me
